Pakistan Single Window

Pakistan Single Window (PSW) is an ICT-based National Single Window (NSW) system in Pakistan. My role as UX Engineer included several responsibilities & problems, which over time, I kept solving

Defined and improved PSW's design language.

Problem
To create a design language that not only stands out but is also accessible and relateable

Solution
Conducted multiple stakeholder meetings to ideate on the brand and the product design direction. The process was documented and design artifacts were created accordingly.

Impact
The activities resulted in a brand that is recognizable and a design language that is now used to convey complex ideas about the product's functions.
